Homeland Security Degrees

Today more than ever, homeland security is one of our nation’s top priorities. At Columbia Southern University, we understand in order to keep our country safe, it must enlist the most dedicated and qualified professionals. That’s why we offer flexible online homeland security degrees to prepare you for a rewarding career keeping your friends, family and neighbors safe.

Taught by some of the industry’s most experienced and respected leaders, students can choose from two bachelor’s degree programs including:

  • B.S. Homeland Security
  • B.S. Information Systems Security – Homeland Security

For more information on CSU’s homeland security degrees, visit ColumbiaSouthern.edu

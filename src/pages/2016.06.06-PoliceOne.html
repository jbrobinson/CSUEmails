Criminal Justice Degrees

For police officers, every day brings a new challenge. At Columbia Southern University, we get it. That’s why we offer flexible online criminal justice degrees that allow you to earn the promotion you deserve at your own pace, while keeping your current job a priority.

Taught by some of the industry’s most experienced and respected leaders, students can choose from associate, bachelor’s or master’s degree programs including:

  • A.S. Criminal Justice
  • B.S. Criminal Justice Administration
  • B.S. Criminal Justice Administration - Arson Investigation
  • M.S. Criminal Justice Administration
  • M.P.A. - Criminal Justice Administration

For more information on degrees or continuing education units in criminal justice, visit ColumbiaSouthern.edu

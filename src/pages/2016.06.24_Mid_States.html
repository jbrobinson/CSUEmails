Join Columbia Southern University and thousands of fire professionals on July 28-29, 2016 at this year’s Mid-States Fire Conference at John Brown University in Siloam Springs, Arkansas.

Speaking Engagements

This year, the conference will feature two days of speaking engagements including topics surrounding fire training, dynamics, the evolution of firefighter responsibility and more.

  • Training for Tomorrow: My Son’s Legacy

    Maj. Brian Arnold, Oklahoma City Fire Department
    Thursday, July 28

  • Fire Research, Tactics, Training and Application

    Robin Zevotek, Lead Research Engineer, Underwriters Laboratories Firefighter Safety Research Institute
    Thursday, July 28

  • Bringing Science to the Streets Through Modern Fire Behavior Training and Tactics

    Capt. James Golondzinier, Los Angeles Fire Department
    Brian Kazmierzak, Chief of Training, Penn Township Fire Department
    Friday, July 29

Doll-House Burn

At the conclusion on the conference, attendees are invited to participate in a live doll-house burn, putting their new skills in action.

The Continuing Education Department of Columbia Southern University is excited to add a new course to our online catalog, CE 1201- School Safety & Security: Next Steps. This course is unlike any other course we offer. Whether you are in Criminal Justice, Emergency Services Management, a city official, a school administrator, public safety officer, or just a community citizen – this course has something relevant for everyone!

 

The School Safety & Security : Next Steps is an awareness/ technical level course designed to promote understanding of the unique operating environments, philosophies, and challenges faced by school campus facilities as they relate to dynamic internal crisis and event situations.

 
Cost: $195
CEUs Earned: 2.3
Prerequisites: None
 

CSU’s Department of Continuing Education is proud to offer courses that allow students the opportunity to enhance their current careers or provide them with relevant training. CEU courses are all open-enrollment and LifePace format.

* Continuing Education courses do not qualify for military benefits, Learning Partner discounts, or Financial Assistance.
 

For more information about this course and to enroll visit www.ColumbiaSouthern.edu/CE1201
